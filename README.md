# Bullion Ledger

Track your gold and silver holdings against live spot prices.

## Support

Having an issue or a question about Bullion Ledger? Please [open an issue](../../issues) on this repo and I'll get back to you.

## What it does

- Log every purchase — gold or silver — with date, weight (oz or grams), and price paid
- Live spot prices for gold (XAU) and silver (XAG), pulled automatically
- See total vault value, cost basis, and gain/loss at a glance
- Filter your holdings by All, Gold, or Silver
- Export your vault to a file as a backup, and import it back in on a new device

## Privacy

Bullion Ledger does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All purchase records you enter are stored locally on your device. The app fetches public gold/silver spot prices over HTTPS; no personal or device data is sent with these requests.
